Settings

Most of the controls on this page allow you to configure the utility to your liking and are self-explanatory. The Eq2.ini section offers some settings that change the behavior of the game.

You can set the in-game Options (Alt+O) window to show advanced options by default, remove the 10-second delay that many players experience when zoning (a known issue with the Examine window), and prevent most of the group chat spam generated by mercenaries.
